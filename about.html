About Me

Biography

I have prior experience in digital marketing, which makes me a strong user advocate leveraging data (qualitative and quantitative) to create innovative solutions that serve both users and business goals. Coming from a marketing background allows me to keep business goals in mind while advocating for the users.



As a UX designer, my mission is to help entrepreneurs or tech start-ups committed to innovative solutions, diversity, and inclusion gain insight into the problems they are experiencing with their digital products and make them more useful to their customers. I accomplish this by resolving their issues uniquely and insightfully and by developing innovative, approachable user experience frameworks.



I enjoy working on projects that improve people's lives by making digital products easier and intuitive to use. The combination of my ux design passion and your vision will result in a fantastic final product.
