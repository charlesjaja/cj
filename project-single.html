Project

Find a better balance between the challenges of health, wellness, work, and life in general.

    Category

    UX Design, UI Design

    Tools

    Adobe XD, Invision, Figma, Adobe Photoshop

    Date

    Nov 2021

CHALLENGE

To develop a digital product that will enable health-conscious individuals to easily stay on top of their wellness, health, and medical needs, as well as get supported on their journey to a more balanced way of living.

OPPORTUNITY

To comprehend the best approach that health-conscious individuals could take to strike a better balance between the challenges of health, wellness, work, and life in general, while also focusing on how we can turn their needs into a superb experience that can potentially drive new customers and increase revenue for the business.

REFLECTION

We live in a digital age where everything is virtual and connected. The problem with this "always-on" lifestyle is that it causes burnout in many people. Add to that the stresses of work and life in general, and it's no surprise that many people struggle to keep up with their health needs or conditions.

Olive assists people in maintaining their health and wellness, as well as finding a better balance between the challenges of health, wellness, work, and life in general. Similar applications frequently involve activities like yoga, mindfulness, and therapy, but Olive also includes practical methods for organising commitments and needs.

In this project, I demonstrated effective methods for engaging health-conscious individuals with an app and how this app could make it much easier to stay on top of wellness, health, and medical needs. I included a lot of analysis to explain the entire design process, such as competition analysis, feature analysis, rapid prototyping for the onboarding flow, interface design that truly meets the needs of users, and much more. Most importantly, I created numerous user personas to evaluate and improve the UX. 


VALIDATING THE SOLUTION
Designed for a specific circumstance:

Many individuals have high-stress jobs and no time for “work-life balance.” This app makes staying on top of wellness, health, and medical needs that much easier.

Designed for inclusivity:

Not everyone is built the same, and users have diverse physical and social circumstances. I considered a design that caters for those with physical, psychological, and social needs.

Educating the user:

Not everyone knows how to change their behaviours or create healthy habits. This app offers support to users to help them on their journey to a more balanced way of living.


WHAT I LEARNT:

As a UX designer, I learned that everything you do must structure in a way that others can easily consume. It is much easier to collaborate and communicate with others when there is structure. This project taught me how to incorporate structure into all aspects of my design process.
