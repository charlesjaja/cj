UX Design

Services

User Research & Analysis

Competitor analysis, web analytics, user interviews, and preliminary user testing will help you understand your users' goals, needs, and mental models.

Persona and Scenario Creation

Analysed data is used to create personas – fictional profiles of your real users – which serve as the foundation for scenarios – patterns of user-software interaction.

User Journey Mapping

Various scenarios are combined to create a comprehensive user journey map that covers all possible points of interaction between users and your software.

Wireframing and Prototyping

The user journey map is converted into a series of wireframes. When you put them together, you get a prototype that gives you a clear vision of a future web or mobile app without requiring you to invest in front-end code development.

Testing

Thorough usability testing of the finished prototype to ensure the quality of the UX design and eliminate the possibility of critical UX errors prior to front-end development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Payment is usually divided into 3 or 4 instalments, but this can be adjusted to meet the needs of the client.

Can I just get a Logo?

No, it does not provide the transformation that I want to provide to my clients. A transformed brand presence is founded on clarity and revelation, which necessitates a lot more preparation. The logo, on its own, cannot convey the tone and personality across all of their touchpoints.

How much does it cost?

Each package is distinct, which is reflected in the varying cost per project. The majority of projects range between €500 and €10,000. The cost can vary depending on the scope. 

How long does it take?

UX research and analysis projects typically take 7 to 10 weeks to complete. Projects may take longer depending on their scope. Taking my time to marinate the ideas is important, so I try to keep a steady but unhurried pace.
